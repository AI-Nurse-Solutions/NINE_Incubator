## 1. Purpose

This document provides a comparative scoring of all eight NINE² clinical and professional niche tracks against the criteria defined in the Playbook. The purpose is to validate the selection of VNT-03 as the anchor niche and to prioritize future fork opportunities. Scores are derived from the Playbook descriptions and initial findings from Research Pulse #001.

## 2. Scoring Criteria (from Playbook)

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Workflow Complexity** | HIGH | Manual steps, handoffs, documentation burden AI can reduce. |
| **Clinical Authority Density** | HIGH | How much nurse judgment is embedded in the workflow. |
| **EDENA Risk Profile** | HIGH | Percentage of workflow that is Green vs. Yellow vs. Red. |
| **Distribution Access** | HIGH | Can you reach 3 real nurses in this niche manually? |
| **Regulatory Risk** | MEDIUM | HIPAA, Joint Commission, state board exposure. |
| **Credentialing Fit** | MEDIUM | Which NAIO certification level is required (C, A, or F). |
| **Expansion Surface** | MEDIUM | Adjacent workflows after winning the first wedge. |
| **Base Reusability** | HIGH | How much of the NINE² base can serve this niche unchanged. |

## 4. Analysis & Interpretation

- **VNT-03 (Nurse Venture Builder)** clearly emerges as the strongest anchor niche with the highest average score (7.8). It combines high authority and complexity with a favorable (low-risk) EDENA profile and maximum reusability of the base incubator. This confirms the initial assessment from INIT-4.

- **EDU-02 (Nurse Educator)** is the second-highest scoring track (7.6). Its favorable Green-tier EDENA profile and low regulatory risk make it an attractive candidate for the second fork after VNT-03 is established.

- **ICU-01 and PER-06**, while having high complexity and authority, score lower due to their high-risk EDENA profiles (heavy Yellow/Red tiers) and significant regulatory overhead. These are powerful but challenging niches that should be tackled after the base governance model is battle-tested in lower-risk environments.

- **STF-08 (Staffing)** presents an interesting opportunity with a very favorable Green-tier profile, but its lower authority density makes it less of a strategic fit for the initial focus on establishing a governance moat.

## 5. Recommendation

Proceed with **VNT-03 (Nurse Venture Builder)** as the anchor niche. Its high score, strategic alignment with the "factory" model, and favorable risk profile make it the ideal choice to build and refine the NINE² Base Incubator.
