## 1. Purpose

This document specifies the multi-stage pricing model for the NINE² Incubator. The model is designed to align the incubator's revenue with the success of its nurse-led ventures, transitioning from an initial access fee to a long-term, outcome-based partnership. This aligns with **Standing Doctrine 4: The venture's success is the incubator's success.**

## 2. Pricing Stages

The model consists of two primary stages: an initial Cohort Access Fee and a subsequent Outcome-Based Fee.

### Stage 1: Cohort Access Fee

**Objective:** To cover the initial, fixed costs of onboarding, infrastructure, and intensive support for a new venture cohort. This fee also serves as a filter to ensure that incoming nurse founders are committed to the program.

| Item | Description | Price |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cohort Seat** | A one-time fee per venture to join a NINE² niche incubator track. | $5,000 USD |

**What it includes:**
-   Access to the full, governed AI agent team for the specific track.
-   Dedicated infrastructure (compute, storage, CI/CD).
-   Initial 12 weeks of intensive, hands-on support from the NAIO Governance Anchor.
-   NAIO-C (Credentialed) training and certification for the founder.

### Stage 2: Outcome-Based Fee

**Objective:** To create a long-term, mutually beneficial financial relationship where NINE² only succeeds when the ventures it supports succeed. This fee is triggered only after the venture achieves specific, pre-defined success milestones.

**Trigger:** The Outcome-Based Fee is activated when a venture achieves one of the following:
1.  Secures its first institutional contract (e.g., a pilot with a hospital).
2.  Raises a seed or Series A funding round of over $250,000 USD.
3.  Is acquired.

**Fee Structure:**

Upon triggering, the venture grants NINE² a **4% equity stake** in the form of common stock or an equivalent instrument. This ensures that NINE²'s financial interests are directly tied to the long-term value creation of the venture.

| Milestone | NINE² Fee |
| :--- | :--- |
| First Institutional Contract | 4% Equity |
| >$250k Seed/Series A Round | 4% Equity |
| Acquisition | 4% of Exit Value |

## 3. Rationale

-   **Low Upfront Barrier:** The initial cohort fee is intentionally kept low to remain accessible to early-stage nurse founders who may be self-funding.
-   **Shared Risk & Reward:** The outcome-based model means that NINE² takes on the majority of the financial risk. We are betting on our ability to help our ventures succeed, and we only get paid if we do.
-   **Long-Term Alignment:** An equity stake ensures that NINE² remains a committed partner long after the initial cohort period, providing ongoing support and governance oversight.
-   **Simplicity:** This two-stage model is simple to understand and communicate, avoiding complex revenue-sharing agreements or other confusing terms.

This pricing model is a direct reflection of our core values. We are not a fee-for-service consultancy; we are a true incubator, and our success is inextricably linked to the success of the nurses we serve.
